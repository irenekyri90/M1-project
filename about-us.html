Welcome to The Cocktail Lab

Develop your mixology skills and create all sorts of drinks for yourself, your significant other, or your friends in the comfort of your own home.

What do you need?

If you're an aspiring mixologist, the first step is having the necessary tools. These are essential for making great cocktails and their quality really does make a difference. Here are 8 essential tools for making amazing cocktails.

A jigger, a bar spoon, a strainer, a muddler, a shaker, a channel knife, a citrus juicer, and glasses.

Now, it's time to choose what cocktail you are going to make!
